Our projects

New builds

We offer a complete design and build service. We corporate with some of the best architects in the county who will work with you to understand your vision and help us in making the best choices for the project. We have considerable experience working on various new build projects. We also specialize in house extensions and loft/garage conversions.

Bathrooms

We have considerable experience in bathroom renovations. Whether it is a small -scale refurbishment or a complete redesign of your bathroom, we are here to assist you. If you have a bathroom design in place, we will do our best to produce it in a way that meets your expectations. If you are ,however, not sure of what would be the best solution for your space, we will provide you with a rich variety of options.

Repairs

There is no job too small for us. We offer a range of home repairs starting from small leaks to roof replacement. We can fix problems with kitchens, baths, walls, floors and garden fencing. We can also offer a discount if you decide to use our services for several issues that need addressing in your house.

Renovations

If you feel like your house needs an upgrade, we can provide you with a wide range of services from a decorating job to larger projects like working on internal structural pieces. We have worked on dilapidated houses and transformed them into beautiful, comfortable spaces.
